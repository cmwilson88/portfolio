About Me

Hello! My name is Christopher Wilson and I am a full stack web developer. I enjoy building web applications with the latest technologies and pushing myself to learn new frameworks and languages.

For more than a year, I have been self-studying web development on my own during down time or after work using a variety of online resources such as Codecademy, Udemy, and FreeCodeCamp in addition to books and podcasts during my morning and afternoon commutes. I also attend two monthly web development meetups in Gainesville, FL (Frontend Awesome and Gainesville JS) in order to immerse myself in the languages and meet like-minded individuals.

During the summer of 2017 I attended DevMountain, a thirteen week immersive web development program in Provo, Utah. In preparation for attending, I decided to prepare as much as possible by learning as much as I could and it paid dividends. While attending, my previous experiences during self-study allowed me to grasp new concepts quicker and helped me fill in gaps in my understanding easier. This allowed me to challenge myself on projects by incorporating additoinal technologies that weren't taught as a part of the curriculum.

When I am not typing away on the latest personal project, I can be found spending time with my lovely wife, watching my favorite sports teams (anything from Cincinnati or the Florida Gators), or learning a new skill (next up, piano!).
